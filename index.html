Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in Physics at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur, advised by Prabhat K. Jaiswal. My research lies at the intersection of numerical simulations and scientific machine learning (SciML) — I develop machine-learning methods to accelerate simulations of phase-ordering kinetics and soft-matter systems.

My thesis, Accelerating numerical simulations using machine learning, explores how neural networks can speed up and augment classical simulation techniques. Before joining IIT Jodhpur, I completed my M.Sc. in Physics at NIT Tiruchirappalli and my B.Sc. in Physics at the University of Delhi.
